About Me

My name is Zack. I'm from Colorado, with half my family originally from Chile. I'm a Ph.D. student at the University of California, Santa Cruz in the Theoretical Computer Science group working under Alex Kolla. I love math, computer science, and the intersection of the two: theoretical computer science.


I did my undergrad at the University of Colorado, Boulder, where I graduated with a BS in computer science and a double minor in applied math and pure math.
